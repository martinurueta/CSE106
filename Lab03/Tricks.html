Tricks

  • Shake

    Difficulty: Easy
    Your dog raises a paw and offers it to you for a shake. This trick is easy to teach and can be a good way to greet guests.
  • Spin

    Difficulty: Medium
    Your dog spins in a circle on command. This trick requires some coordination, but can be a fun way to entertain your dog and impress your friends.
  • Play Dead

    Difficulty: Hard
    Your dog lies down on command and plays dead, usually by rolling onto their back and sticking their legs in the air. This trick takes some time and patience to teach, but can be a crowd-pleaser.
  • Weave

    Difficulty: Advanced
    Your dog weaves in and out of your legs as you walk, like a figure-eight. This trick requires a lot of coordination and training, and is best for experienced dog owners and handlers.
